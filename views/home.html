About the Internship

We are searching for a motivated student for our front-end development internship. Do you want to gain more front-end experience, and learn from experts in the field? Are you passionate about solving user interface and UX problems in a large-scale environment? Do you want to be a part of a fast-paced organization and contribute to one of the most visited sites on the Internet? and you love the Pacific Northwest?

If this describes you, consider joining us as an intern in the summer of 2023. The P.N.W is looking for a front-end engineer intern to join one of our many lines of business.

More about the Us

The P.N.W is committed to becoming a fully inclusive, anti-racist, multicultural organization. To fulfill our brand promise of enabling a life well-lived outside for everyone, we are seeking candidates who demonstrate shared values of diversity, equity, inclusion, and anti-racism.


What You’ll Bring

We want to make sure you get compensated for your skills and hard work. To be qualified for this role, you must have the following:

  • Currently working towards a Bachelor’s Degree in computer science, computer engineering, human-computer interaction, or other equivalent technical disciplines with an expected conferral date between October 2023 – September 2026.
  • Proficiency with best practices in HTML, CSS and/or JavaScript.
  • Experience in, at least, one modern programming language such as HTML, CSS, Javascript, PHP.
  • Strong problem-solving and critical thinking skills
  • Proficiency with Java, SQL or other tools is a plus
  • Self-motivated, able to work independently but also collaborative and driven by group critique
  • Professional and positive communication skills
